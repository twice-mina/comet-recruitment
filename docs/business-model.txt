1. The business model
What Prompt Academy Jobs is

A niche jobs platform that:

curates real external jobs

lets users browse them like a normal job board

requires Prompt Academy AI proficiency screening before applying through your channel

certifies candidates who meet your standard

may vouch for or submit certified candidates through your application channel

recommends training to candidates who do not meet the standard yet

What it is not

It is not:

pretending Prompt Academy is the employer

saying the employer requires the certificate unless that is actually true

guaranteeing interviews or offers

forcing a misleading pay-to-apply setup

The operating idea is:

“This is a real external job. Prompt Academy has its own application channel and only allows certified candidates through that channel.”

2. The product architecture

You really have four products in one:

A. Jobs marketplace

Public-facing job discovery experience.

B. AI proficiency screening engine

Your gatekeeper layer that determines whether someone can proceed through your application channel.

C. Certification layer

The formal output of passing your proficiency bar.

D. Candidate pipeline / talent network

Where certified candidates are tracked, ranked, and potentially submitted or vouched for.

That means the site should be built around these main areas:

4. The end-to-end user flow

There are really three major user paths.

Path 1: Qualified candidate who passes

lands on jobs page

browses listings

opens job detail page

clicks “Apply Through Prompt Academy”

sees application requirement page

completes AI proficiency screening

passes

gets Prompt Academy certification

completes application

enters candidate dashboard

application is reviewed / submitted / referred

receives updates and next steps

Path 2: Candidate who fails but is still valuable

lands on jobs page

opens role

tries to apply

completes screening

fails threshold

sees “not yet eligible through Prompt Academy channel”

receives specific skill-gap feedback

sees recommended courses / training

enrolls in training

retakes screening

passes later

applies

Path 3: Candidate who does not want your channel

lands on job page

reads full job

decides not to go through Prompt Academy screening

either leaves or uses direct external apply route if you choose to offer one

That third path is strategically useful because it makes the platform feel more real and less coercive.

15. The operating logic behind “vouching”

This is important. “Vouching” should become a real internal process.

What vouching can mean operationally

Prompt Academy may:

certify that a candidate met your AI threshold

provide a standardized candidate summary

present candidate strengths

flag candidate role fit

optionally forward candidate to recruiter/employer contact if relationship exists

Internal vouch score

You may want:

This helps you avoid treating every pass the same.

18. The strongest messaging hierarchy

You want the message stack to go in this order:

First message

These are real, relevant jobs.

Second message

Prompt Academy has a distinct application channel.

Third message

That channel requires AI proficiency screening and certification.

Fourth message

Certified candidates may proceed and may be vouched for by Prompt Academy.

Fifth message

If you do not qualify yet, we will tell you how to improve.

That sequence is psychologically much stronger than leading with “buy certification.”
